# CodeBook
This codebook provides a brief summary on the data in `tidy.txt`

## ID's / Key fields
* `Subject_ID` - The subject ID / Identifies the person who participated in the experiment (Values `1` to `30`)
* `Activity` - The name/label of the activity performed by the respective subject

## Activity Labels (Value - Name)
* `1` `WALKING`
* `2` `WALKING UPSTAIRS`
* `3` `WALKING DOWNSTAIRS`
* `4` `SITTING`
* `5` `STANDING`
* `6` `LAYING`
